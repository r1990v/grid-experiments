Goodbye

Man Utd 3-2 Southampton

One of THE great cup finals.

  • Jose Mourinho joins legends Sir Alex Ferguson and Brian Clough in winning the League Cup four times.
  • Mourinho becomes the first Manchester United manager to win a major trophy in his first season.
  • Mourinho keeps up a 100% winning record in English domestic finals.

But it is Zlatan Ibrahimovic's name which will be at the top of the headlines after the Swede scored twice, including a late winner, to give United victory over Southampton.
